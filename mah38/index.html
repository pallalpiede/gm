    Help

    Game info

    MahJong is a high-quality collection of MahJong solitaire games.

    Rules

    Playing Rules

    • 'N' pairs of tiles are randomly dealt face down on the layout grid.
    • Your goal is to find and remove all the matching pairs of tiles.
    • You can uncover two tiles at a time by clicking on them in any order you wish.
    • When you find a pair, the tiles are automatically removed from the layout; otherwise they are turned face down.

    Scoring

    • The game starts at: 0 (zero).
    • 50 points are awarded for each pair matched.

    Game Won

    • You win if you remove all the tiles

    Strategy

    • It is suggested to finish all the couples of the upper level and then continue with the lower levels. Otherwise the game could be not winnable.
